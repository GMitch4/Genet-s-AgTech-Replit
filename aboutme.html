A New York native with a passion for healing and educating through nature and city pizazz. Born in Harlem, Genét took in her environment by immersing herself into hustle and bustle of the city while enjoying the subtle presence of nature. Her love for nature took her into the holistic path of herbalism and gardening.

As she continues to broaden her horizons in soil-less food systems, Genet wishes to curate community food boxes to provide to anyone who suffers from food security. People should not be without food, when community exists! Her lifetime goal is to be able to have three functioning sustainable food systems in at-least three states.
